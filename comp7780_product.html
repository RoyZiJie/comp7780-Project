About Green World

🌱 Green World is a Hong Kong-based recycling social enterprise, established on January 1, 2026. All products in this e-shop are donated by the public and sold to you at discounted prices.

♻️ Our mission: Reduce waste, extend product life, and build a circular economy. Every purchase you make directly supports our environmental education programs and local recycling initiatives.

📦 How it works: Donate your unwanted items → We refurbish & list them → You buy at low price → Together we help the planet.
